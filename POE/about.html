About Mzansi Fresh Market

Our History

Mzansi Fresh Market was born from a simple idea: bring quality, affordable groceries to the heart of South African communities. Founded in 2025 by a group of township entrepreneurs and tech-savvy visionaries, the first store opened in Soweto with crates of fresh produce, a kota stand, and a mission to uplift local suppliers. What started as a single outlet quickly grew into a network of community-powered markets across Gauteng, KwaZulu-Natal, and the Eastern Cape.

Our Mission

To empower South African communities by providing access to fresh, affordable food, supporting local farmers and traders, and delivering everyday convenience through smart, inclusive retail.

Our Vision

To become South Africa's most trusted township retail brand, blending tradition, innovation, and community pride—one market at a time.
